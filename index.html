Take a Deep Breath

A simple webpage with tips and exercises to support mental well-being.

Breathing Exercises

4-7-8 Technique

Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 7 seconds, exhale for 8 seconds. Repeat a few times.

Daily Tips

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of water to keep your body and mind fresh.

Take a Break

Stretch or walk for 5 minutes to refresh your focus.

Write it Down

Journaling your thoughts for 10 minutes can help reduce stress.
